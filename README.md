# Gmod Teach GMA

**What is this project?**

GMOD-TEACH-GMA is a open-source software/utility/project dedicated to the garrysmods community. It simply allows for the automatic and easy installation of manual mod files.

---

**How to use it?**

Its operation and use are quite simple; you only need to provide two inputs (folders/addresses on your computer). You must open the config.ini file and fill in the fields:

``GMAD-LS=`` your/address/where/are-your.gma-o-bin

``GMOD-DIR=`` your/address/where/is-your-"Garrysmods/garrysmods"

>(without spaces)

Once that's done, it's as easy as running ``GMoD-TeachGMAD.exe`` and press enter to have everything installed automatically , Yes, the .bin files too.

---

**Notes**

There's still no system for naming and organizing the resulting folders after mod installation (meaning your mods end up in rather disorganized add-ons, with several even sharing generic folders). Of course, this will be significantly improved in future updates. But if you're a programmer, you can always share your ideas through the Discord channel or by contacting me via the email in my profile—the old-fashioned way.
